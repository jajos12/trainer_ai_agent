AI Agents Training

1. Overview and Key Concepts

Artificial intelligence (AI) agents are autonomous entities that perceive their en vironment and act upon it to achieve specific goals. Unlike traditional programs, AI age nts learn and adapt through experience, using various techniques like reinforcement learn ing, supervised learning, or a combination thereof. They are characterized by their abil ity to:

  • Perceive: Gather information from their environment th rough sensors (e.g., cameras, sensors, APIs).
  • Reason: Process inform ation and make decisions based on their goals and knowledge.
  • Act: Ta ke actions in their environment to influence outcomes.
  • Learn: Adapt their behavior based on feedback and experience.

AI agents find applicati ons in diverse fields, including robotics, game playing, autonomous driving, and resource management. Recent research highlights the increasing sophistication of AI agents, driv en by advancements in large language models (LLMs) and deep learning.
